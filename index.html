Answers to common questions about Future Skills Forum 2025.

Future Skills Forum 2025 is a one-day conference focused on AI, digital transformation, and the future workforce. You will hear from leaders who are already re-shaping how people learn and work.

The forum is ideal for HR and L&D leaders, business executives, team managers, educators, and anyone responsible for preparing people for AI-powered, digital-first work.

Sessions will cover practical AI use cases at work, reskilling and upskilling strategies, digital literacy, leadership in the age of automation, and how to design future-ready learning programs.

Future Skills Forum 2025 is an in-person event at Jakarta Convention Center with selected sessions streamed online. All registered participants will receive details in their confirmation email.
